Cat Facts


Random Cat Facts:


Here are some more interesting facts:

  • Cats have 3 eyelids.
  • Cats typically live between 15-20 years.
  • Cats don't have tastebuds for sweet things.
  • Cats can jump as far as 6 times their lenth.
  • Domestic cats can run up to 30 miles/hour.
  • There are around 70 different breeds of cats.
  • A cat's brain is 90% similar to a human's brain.
  • On average, cats sleep for 12-14 hours every day.
  • Maine Coons are the largest domesticated cat breed.
  • Every cat's nose is as unique as a human's fingerprint.
  • Cats can hear high pitched sounds that humans cannot hear.
  • Cats sweat through their paws as a way to mark their territory.
  • Cats have 5 toes on their front paws, and only 4 toes on their back paws.
  • Adult cats only meow to communicate with humans, not with each other.
  • Cats have 32 muscles in their ears, compared to humans who only have 6.
  • Cats can make 100 different sounds, compared to dogs who can only make 10.
  • In America, there is an estimated 88 million cats, compared to 74 million dogs.
  • It is believed that cats were first domesticated around 8,000 years ago in Egypt.
  • A cat's sense of smell is around 14 times stronger than a human's sense of smell.
  • A cat's field of view is 220 degrees, compared to humans who can only see up to 180 degrees.
