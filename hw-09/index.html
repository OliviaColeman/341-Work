Zombie Definition

What is a Zombie?



A zombie is a reanimated human corpse. The term zombie originated from Haitian Folklore, and was popularized by science fiction. It was originally believed that zombies were created through witchcraft and voodoo. The voodoo priestess would reanimae the corpse and use it as a personal slave. Today, zombies are usually thought to be created by a virus, disease, or radiation. These zombies crave human flesh and brains.
